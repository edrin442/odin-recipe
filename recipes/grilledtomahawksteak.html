Lasagna Recipes

Description

Both vegetables and salade are important to maintain wealth

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cucumbers
  • Radishes
  • Red and Green Pappers

Directions

  1. Gather your Ingredients
  2. Wash Vegetables
  3. Chop the Vegetables
  4. Prepare any addition Ingredients
  5. Combine the Ingredients
  6. Add dressing
  7. Toss the salade
  8. Serve and Enjoy the Meal
